Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How quickly can you respond to emergency repair requests?

Our emergency response team aims to be on-site within 2 hours of your call for critical issues. Response times may vary based on location and current workload.

Do you offer preventive maintenance contracts?

Yes, we offer customized preventive maintenance contracts tailored to your specific equipment and facility needs. These help prevent unexpected breakdowns and extend asset lifespan.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve the Northern Cape region with our maintenance and repair services. For supply chain and distribution services, we operate throughout South Africa.

Are your technicians certified and insured?

Yes, all our technicians are properly certified for their respective trades and fully insured. We maintain high standards for both technical competence and safety.

How do you handle after-hours emergencies?

We have a dedicated 24/7 emergency hotline for urgent repair needs. Our on-call technicians are available around the clock to address critical issues.

Can you provide references from past clients?

Absolutely. We're proud of our track record and can provide references from satisfied clients across various industries upon request.
